## 1Ô∏è‚É£ SITEGROUND AFFILIATE PROGRAM (PRIORITY)

**Why Start Here:** You already use SiteGround, so you know the product well. Commission: $50-$100 per sale.

### Step-by-Step Signup:

1. **Visit the Signup Page**
   - Go to: https://www.siteground.com/affiliates
   - Click "Join Our Affiliate Program"

2. **Fill Out Application Form**
   - **Email:** Use a professional email (avoid free Gmail/Yahoo if possible)
   - **Full Name:** Your legal name (required for payments)
   - **Website:** Enter your Content Flow app URL or business website
   - **How will you promote SiteGround?**
     - Example answer: "Through a WordPress content creation SaaS platform that helps users set up and manage blogs. We recommend hosting providers during the setup process and provide direct links to hosting services."

3. **Describe Your Traffic Sources**
   - Example: "SaaS application users, WordPress bloggers, content creators, small business owners setting up their first website"

4. **Approval Process**
   - SiteGround typically approves within 24-48 hours
   - You'll receive an email with login credentials

5. **After Approval - Get Your Affiliate Link**
   - Log into your SiteGround affiliate dashboard
   - Navigate to "Banners & Links" or "Marketing Materials"
   - Copy your unique affiliate link (format: `https://www.siteground.com/go/[YOUR_ID]`)
   - **SAVE THIS LINK** - you'll need it to update the app

6. **Set Up Payment**
   - Go to Account Settings ‚Üí Payment Information
   - Add your PayPal email (recommended) or bank details
   - Complete tax form (W-9 for US, W-8 for international)

### Expected Commission Structure:
- 1-5 sales/month: **$50 per sale**
- 6-10 sales/month: **$75 per sale**
- 11-20 sales/month: **$100 per sale**
- Cookie Duration: 60 days
- Payout: Weekly (every Wednesday), 30-40 days after sale

## 2Ô∏è‚É£ BLUEHOST AFFILIATE PROGRAM

**Why Bluehost:** WordPress.org officially recommends them. Commission: $65-$100 per sale, 120-day cookie.

### Step-by-Step Signup:

1. **Visit the Signup Page**
   - Go to: https://www.bluehost.com/affiliates
   - Click "Join Now" or "Sign Up"

2. **Complete Application**
   - They use Impact Radius as their affiliate network
   - You'll create an account on Impact's platform
   - Fill in business information:
     - Company Name: Your business name
     - Website: Your Content Flow URL
     - Promotional Methods: "SaaS application, email marketing, content marketing"

3. **Describe Your Promotion Strategy**
   - Example: "We operate a WordPress content management platform that guides users through blog setup. We recommend hosting providers to users who don't have websites yet, with direct affiliate links during the onboarding process."

4. **Approval Time**
   - Usually 2-3 business days
   - May require follow-up if they have questions

5. **After Approval - Get Tracking Link**
   - Log into Impact Radius dashboard
   - Find Bluehost in your programs
   - Navigate to "Links" or "Marketing Materials"
   - Generate your tracking link
   - Format is usually: `https://www.bluehost.com/track/[YOUR_ID]`
   - You can add custom parameters: `?utm_source=contentflow&utm_medium=affiliate`

6. **Payment Setup**
   - Set up payment in Impact Radius dashboard
   - Options: PayPal, direct deposit, Payoneer
   - Complete tax documentation

### Expected Commission:
- **$65 base commission** per hosting sale
- Can increase to **$100** for high performers
- Cookie Duration: 120 days (longest in the industry!)
- Payout: Net-45 (45 days after sale)

## üí° PRO TIPS FOR APPROVAL

### Increase Your Approval Chances:

1. **Use a Professional Email**
   - Business domain email is better than Gmail
   - Shows you're serious about the partnership

2. **Have a Website/Landing Page**
   - If you don't have one, use your Content Flow app URL
   - Or create a simple landing page explaining your service

3. **Be Specific About Promotion Methods**
   - Don't say "social media" - be specific
   - Mention your SaaS platform, the onboarding process, user base
   - Emphasize that users are actively looking for hosting (high intent)

4. **Traffic Numbers**
   - If asked, you can estimate based on current/projected users
   - Be honest but optimistic
   - Example: "Currently 50-100 users monthly, growing to 500+ users within 6 months"

5. **Follow Up**
   - If not approved within a week, contact their affiliate support
   - Ask if they need additional information
